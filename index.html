Organic Compound Interactive Diagram

Instructions:

1. Select an element and click on the text to edit. You can also drag it around.

2. Click on the arrows after selecting an element to create new linked elements.

3. Click on the bonds to change between single, double, and triple bonds.

4. Select an element and click the 'Change charge' button below to change its charge.

5. To add ions for salts (eg. Na+), click on the 'Add Ion' button below.

6. Drag the ion beside the element you want it to connect to. It should snap into place.

7. Delete unwanted ions or elements with BACKSPACE or the 'Delete element' button below.

Tip 1: You can zoom in and out of the diagram. Use the + and - buttons on a computer or pinch in and out on mobile.

Tip 2: After an ion snaps into place, the charges will be circled to indicate an ionic bond.

Tip 3: You can hold SHIFT and drag a connected ion if you want to disconnect and reposition it.
